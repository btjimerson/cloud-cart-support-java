# Warranty Policy

## Coverage

Agentic Cart stands behind the quality of the products we sell. Our warranty policy covers manufacturing defects and workmanship issues for eligible products purchased through our store. This warranty is in addition to any manufacturer warranties that may apply to your product.

### What Is Covered

Our warranty covers:
- Manufacturing defects in materials or workmanship
- Parts that fail under normal use within the warranty period
- Functional defects that prevent the product from operating as designed
- Electronic components that fail during normal operation
- Mechanical components that break or malfunction with proper use

### What Is NOT Covered

This warranty does not cover:
- Normal wear and tear or cosmetic damage
- Damage from misuse, abuse, or negligence
- Damage from accidents, drops, or impacts
- Damage from unauthorized repairs or modifications
- Damage from improper installation or use
- Damage from exposure to extreme temperatures, moisture, or environmental conditions
- Batteries and other consumable components (unless defective within first 30 days)
- Software issues or compatibility problems
- Damage from power surges or electrical issues (unless product has built-in surge protection)
- Loss of data or indirect damages
- Products purchased from unauthorized resellers

### Product Categories

Different product categories have different warranty terms:

#### Electronics
- Computers, monitors, cameras, audio equipment
- Defects in hardware components
- Does not cover software, scratches on screens, or worn cables

#### Appliances
- Kitchen appliances, home appliances
- Mechanical and electrical failures
- Does not cover glass, accessories, or consumable filters

#### Tools and Equipment
- Power tools, hand tools, outdoor equipment
- Manufacturing defects in motor, switches, or housing
- Does not cover blades, bits, or other replaceable parts

#### Fitness Equipment
- Exercise machines, weights, training equipment
- Structural defects and mechanical failures
- Does not cover wear on grips, pads, or elastic components

#### Outdoor Gear
- Camping equipment, outdoor accessories
- Defects in materials like rips, broken zippers, or frame failures
- Does not cover fading, stains, or damage from outdoor conditions

## Claims Process

### Step 1: Determine Warranty Status
Before filing a claim:
- Verify your purchase date and warranty period in your order history
- Review warranty terms for your specific product category
- Determine if the issue is covered under warranty terms
- Gather proof of purchase (order number and receipt)

### Step 2: Contact Customer Service
To initiate a warranty claim:
- Email: warranty@agenticcart.com with order number and description of issue
- Phone: 1-800-CART-123 (Monday-Friday, 9 AM - 6 PM EST)
- Provide photos or video of the defect when possible
- Include product model number and purchase date
- Describe the issue and how the product was being used

### Step 3: Claim Review
Our warranty team will:
- Review your claim within 2 business days
- May request additional information or photos
- Determine if issue is covered under warranty terms
- Provide claim decision and next steps via email

### Step 4: Resolution Options

#### Option A: Replacement
- We will ship a replacement product at no cost
- No need to return defective product in most cases
- Replacement ships within 3-5 business days of approval
- Replaced product carries remainder of original warranty period

#### Option B: Repair
- For certain products, repair may be offered instead of replacement
- We will provide prepaid shipping label for return
- Repairs completed within 10-15 business days
- Repaired product returned via expedited shipping

#### Option C: Refund
- For products no longer available or unrepairable
- Refund issued for original purchase price
- May be issued as store credit at customer's preference
- Original shipping costs not refunded

### Step 5: Return Process (if required)
If product return is necessary:
- Use prepaid shipping label provided by warranty team
- Package product securely in original packaging when possible
- Include warranty claim number on outside of package
- Ship within 14 days of receiving return authorization
- Keep tracking information for your records

### Documentation Required
For warranty claims, please provide:
- Order number or proof of purchase
- Product model number and serial number (if applicable)
- Photos clearly showing the defect or issue
- Description of when and how the issue occurred
- Any troubleshooting steps already attempted

### Troubleshooting First
Before filing a warranty claim, please:
- Review product manual and troubleshooting guide
- Check our FAQ and support articles online
- Ensure product is properly assembled and configured
- Verify all cables and connections are secure
- Try fresh batteries or different power source
Many issues can be resolved without a warranty claim.

## Exclusions

### Specific Exclusions by Product Type

#### Electronics
- Pixel defects on displays (unless exceeding manufacturer specification)
- Software bugs or compatibility issues
- Data loss or corruption
- Bluetooth/WiFi connectivity issues not due to hardware failure
- Scratched screens or cosmetic blemishes

#### Batteries and Power
- Diminished battery capacity over time (normal degradation)
- Batteries that fail due to improper charging
- Power adapters damaged by electrical surges
- Batteries swollen due to heat exposure

#### Accessories and Consumables
- Cables that fray with regular use
- Filters that need regular replacement
- Ink, toner, and other consumables
- Protective cases and covers
- Memory cards and storage media

#### Wear Items
- Blades, bits, and cutting tools
- Sandpaper and abrasives
- Belts and drive components
- Gaskets and seals
- Grips, handles, and pads

### Voided Warranty Conditions
Your warranty will be void if:
- Product has been opened or repaired by unauthorized service center
- Serial number has been removed or altered
- Product has been modified or altered from original design
- Damage was caused by unauthorized accessories or parts
- Product was used for commercial purposes when sold for residential use
- Evidence of intentional damage or abuse

### Limitations
- Warranty is non-transferable and valid only for original purchaser
- Proof of purchase from Agentic Cart required for all claims
- We reserve right to replace defective products with comparable models if original is discontinued
- Replacement products may be new or refurbished at our discretion
- We are not liable for incidental or consequential damages
- Shipping costs for warranty returns are covered; original shipping is not refundable
