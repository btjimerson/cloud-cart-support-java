# Shipping Policy

## Shipping Methods

At Agentic Cart, we offer several shipping options to meet your delivery needs. Shipping methods and costs are calculated at checkout based on your location, package weight, and selected service level.

### Domestic Shipping (United States)

#### Standard Shipping
- Delivery Time: 5-7 business days
- Cost: $5.99 flat rate for orders under $50
- Free for orders $50 and above
- Tracking included
- No signature required
- Available for all 50 states including Alaska and Hawaii

#### Expedited Shipping
- Delivery Time: 2-3 business days
- Cost: $12.99 flat rate
- Tracking included
- No signature required
- Not available for PO Boxes
- Orders must be placed by 2 PM EST for same-day processing

#### Express Shipping
- Delivery Time: 1-2 business days
- Cost: $24.99 flat rate
- Tracking included
- Signature required for orders over $200
- Not available for PO Boxes or APO/FPO addresses
- Orders must be placed by 12 PM EST for same-day processing

#### Next Day Air
- Delivery Time: Next business day by 3 PM
- Cost: $39.99 flat rate
- Tracking included
- Signature required
- Not available for PO Boxes, APO/FPO addresses, or Alaska/Hawaii
- Orders must be placed by 12 PM EST for next-day delivery
- Saturday delivery available for additional $15

### Special Handling

#### Large/Heavy Items
Items over 50 lbs or oversized packages may require:
- Freight shipping with additional charges
- Curbside delivery (driver will not bring inside)
- Extended delivery time (7-14 business days)
- Delivery appointment scheduling
- You will be notified of any special handling requirements before shipment

#### Restricted Locations
Some addresses may have shipping restrictions:
- PO Boxes: Standard shipping only
- APO/FPO/DPO: Standard shipping only, allow 10-15 business days
- Alaska/Hawaii: Extended delivery times, additional fees may apply
- Remote areas: May require additional 2-3 days for delivery

## Delivery Times

### Processing Time
- In-stock items: 1-2 business days
- Made-to-order items: 3-5 business days (noted on product page)
- Pre-order items: Ships on release date specified on product page
- Business days are Monday through Friday, excluding holidays

### Transit Time
Transit time begins after the order is processed and shipped. It does not include processing time.

### Estimated Delivery
The estimated delivery date shown at checkout includes both processing and transit time. This is an estimate, not a guarantee. Actual delivery may vary due to carrier delays, weather, or other unforeseen circumstances.

### Holidays and Peak Seasons
During major holidays and peak shopping seasons (Black Friday, Cyber Monday, December holidays):
- Processing times may extend to 2-4 business days
- Carrier delays are more common
- We recommend ordering earlier and selecting expedited shipping for time-sensitive deliveries
- Specific holiday shipping deadlines will be posted on our website

### Address Validation
Orders are shipped to the address provided at checkout:
- Ensure your shipping address is complete and accurate
- We cannot modify the address after the order has been processed
- Address corrections by the carrier may result in additional fees or delays
- Refused or undeliverable packages due to incorrect addresses may be subject to restocking fees

## Tracking

### Tracking Information
- All shipments include tracking numbers
- Tracking numbers are emailed to you within 24 hours of shipment
- You can also view tracking information in your account under Order History

### Tracking Updates
- Tracking information is updated by the carrier, not by Agentic Cart
- Initial tracking scans may take 24-48 hours to appear in the carrier's system
- Tracking shows "label created" status until the package is picked up by the carrier
- For questions about in-transit packages, contact the carrier directly using your tracking number

### Carrier Partners
We ship via the following carriers:
- UPS (primary carrier for most orders)
- FedEx (backup carrier and expedited orders)
- USPS (PO Boxes and some residential addresses)
- Freight carriers for oversized items (varies by region)

### Delivery Issues
If tracking shows delivered but you haven't received your package:
- Check with neighbors or household members
- Look for delivery in alternate locations (porch, garage, building office)
- Wait 24 hours as carriers sometimes scan packages early
- Contact the carrier to open a delivery investigation
- Contact our customer service if the package cannot be located after 3 business days

### Lost or Stolen Packages
- We are not responsible for packages confirmed delivered by the carrier
- We recommend using expedited shipping with signature confirmation for high-value orders
- Consider shipping to a secure location or carrier hold facility
- File a police report for stolen packages and contact our customer service for assistance
- We will work with you and the carrier to investigate and resolve issues

## International Shipping

### Available Countries
We currently ship to:
- Canada
- United Kingdom
- European Union member states
- Australia
- Mexico
- Select countries in Asia and South America (view full list at checkout)

### International Shipping Methods

#### International Standard
- Delivery Time: 10-20 business days
- Cost: Calculated by weight and destination
- Tracking included
- No signature required for orders under $200 USD

#### International Express
- Delivery Time: 3-7 business days
- Cost: Calculated by weight and destination
- Tracking included
- Signature required
- Customs clearance priority

### Customs and Duties

#### Customer Responsibility
- International customers are responsible for all customs duties, taxes, and brokerage fees
- These fees are determined by your country's customs authority
- Fees are collected by the carrier upon delivery or by customs directly
- We cannot estimate these fees - contact your local customs office for information

#### Customs Documentation
- All packages include commercial invoices and customs declarations
- We declare the full retail value of items - we cannot mark packages as "gifts" or declare lower values
- Accurate product descriptions are provided for customs review

#### Customs Delays
- Customs processing can add 3-10 days to delivery time
- We have no control over customs processing times
- Customers are responsible for responding to customs inquiries
- Packages may be returned to us if customs cannot be cleared - return shipping fees apply

### Restrictions
Certain items cannot be shipped internationally:
- Lithium batteries (standalone or in devices)
- Aerosols and pressurized containers
- Liquids over 100ml
- Items containing magnets
- Products with country-specific voltage requirements
International restrictions will be noted on product pages and at checkout.

## Shipping Costs

### Calculation
Shipping costs are calculated based on:
- Package weight and dimensions
- Destination address
- Selected shipping method
- Item type (standard vs. oversized)

### Free Shipping Promotions
- Free shipping promotions apply to standard shipping only within the US
- Minimum order amounts apply before taxes and after discounts
- Cannot be combined with other shipping discounts
- Expedited and express shipping upgrades available for additional cost
- Exclusions may apply to certain products or categories

### Tax and Duties
- US orders: Sales tax applied based on ship-to state requirements
- International orders: Taxes and duties are customer responsibility and not included in checkout total

### Order Splitting
If your order must be split into multiple shipments:
- You will only be charged shipping once
- Each shipment will have its own tracking number
- Items may arrive on different days
- All shipments will be sent to the same address

### Address Changes
- Address changes can be requested within 1 hour of order placement
- Contact customer service immediately to request changes
- Once the order is processed, address changes may not be possible
- Carrier address corrections may incur additional fees charged by the carrier
