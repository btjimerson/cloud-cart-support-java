# Return Policy

## Overview

At Agentic Cart, we want you to be completely satisfied with your purchase. If you're not happy with your order for any reason, our return policy makes it easy to return eligible items for a full refund or exchange. This policy outlines the conditions, process, and timelines for returns.

## Eligibility

### Time Frame
- Items must be returned within 30 days of the delivery date
- The return window begins on the day you receive your order
- For gift purchases, the recipient has 30 days from delivery to initiate a return

### Item Condition
Items must meet the following conditions to be eligible for return:
- Unused and in original condition
- Original packaging intact and included
- All accessories, manuals, and parts included
- No signs of wear, damage, or alteration
- Product tags and labels still attached (where applicable)

### Non-Returnable Items
The following items cannot be returned:
- Items marked as "Final Sale" or "Non-Returnable" at time of purchase
- Opened software, digital downloads, or license keys
- Personalized or custom-made items
- Intimate or sanitary goods (earbuds, personal care items) if packaging has been opened
- Gift cards
- Perishable goods or hazardous materials

## Process

### Step 1: Initiate Return Request
- Log into your Agentic Cart account
- Navigate to Order History and select the order containing items you wish to return
- Click "Request Return" and select the items and reason for return
- You will receive a Return Authorization (RA) number via email within 24 hours

### Step 2: Package Your Return
- Securely package the item(s) in the original packaging when possible
- Include all accessories, manuals, and components
- Print the prepaid return shipping label provided in your RA email
- Clearly write the RA number on the outside of the package

### Step 3: Ship Your Return
- Attach the prepaid return label to your package
- Drop off the package at any authorized carrier location
- Keep your tracking receipt for your records
- International customers are responsible for return shipping costs

### Step 4: Track Your Return
- You can track your return status in your account under Order History
- Once we receive your return, we'll inspect it within 2-3 business days
- You'll receive an email confirmation once your return is processed

## Refund Timeline

### Processing Time
- Returns are typically processed within 2-3 business days of receipt at our warehouse
- You will receive an email notification when your return has been approved

### Refund Method
- Refunds are issued to the original payment method used for purchase
- Gift card purchases will be refunded as store credit
- If original payment method is no longer valid, store credit will be issued

### Refund Timing by Payment Type
- Credit/Debit Cards: 5-7 business days after approval
- PayPal: 3-5 business days after approval
- Store Credit: Immediately upon approval
- Bank Transfers: 7-10 business days after approval

### Shipping Costs
- Original shipping charges are non-refundable unless the return is due to our error
- Return shipping is free when using our prepaid label (US customers only)
- If you return part of an order that qualified for free shipping, original shipping charges may be deducted from your refund

## Exceptions

### Damaged or Defective Items
If you receive a damaged or defective item:
- Contact customer service immediately with photos of the damage
- We will provide a prepaid return label at no cost
- You may choose a replacement or full refund including original shipping
- No restocking fees apply to damaged or defective returns

### Wrong Item Received
If you receive the wrong item:
- Contact customer service within 7 days of delivery
- We will arrange for pickup or provide a prepaid return label
- We will ship the correct item at no additional cost
- Full refund of shipping charges if you prefer not to receive the correct item

### Late or Missing Refunds
If you haven't received your refund within the expected timeframe:
- Check your bank account or credit card statement
- Contact your payment provider - processing times may vary
- Contact our customer service team if the refund is still missing after 10 business days

### Exchanges
- Direct exchanges are not currently available
- To exchange an item, please return it for a refund and place a new order
- This ensures you receive your desired item as quickly as possible

### Warranty Returns
Items being returned under manufacturer warranty should follow the warranty claims process outlined in our Warranty Policy rather than our standard return process. See the Warranty Policy document for details.
